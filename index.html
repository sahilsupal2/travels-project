Featured Destinations

Hampi, Karnataka

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring the ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire. It is an open-air museum with over 500 monuments, including the iconic Virupaksha Temple and Vithala Temple.

Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu

A coastal town famous for its 7th and 8th-century Pallava monuments. Highlights include the Shore Temple, the monolithic Pancha Rathas, and the giant open-air relief known as Arjuna's Penance.

Amer Fort, Rajasthan

A hilltop UNESCO World Heritage site known for its Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace).
